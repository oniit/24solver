Bomber Game

by Mathifyx

You choose to play as either the President or the Terrorists. Each side sends either a President, Bomber, or Member to the enemy base. The Terrorists win if the President and Bomber end up in the same base, while the President wins if the Bomber is kept away, even if the President is in any base.
(The bot's input will be randomly generated.)
